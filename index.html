How it Works
📱 Use it as an app
On mobile, tap Share (iOS) or menu (Android) and select "Add to Home Screen" — installs with its own icon.
Creating a Room (Host)
  • Enter your name, room name, and budget per team (in Lakhs)
  • Optionally enable a bid timer
  • Share the 6-character room code with friends
Joining a Room (Participant)
  • Enter your name and the room code
  • Pick a team name and colour
  • Wait for the host to start
During the Auction
  • The host puts up one player at a time
  • Any team can bid — each bid raises the price by a fixed increment
  • Highest bidder when time runs out wins the player
  • Watch your remaining budget in the top bar
Squad Rules
  • Host sets the max squad size: 16, 18 or 20 players
  • Category limits — 16-man: 6 Bat / 6 Bowl / 4 AR / 2 WK  |  18-man: 7/7/4/2  |  20-man: 7/7/5/3
  • Once a category is full, you cannot bid on players of that role
  • Track your counts in the Squad tab during the auction
Wrapping Up
  • When all players are sold, the host ends the auction
  • Pick your Captain (2× points) and Vice-Captain (1.5×) — one-time choice for the whole tournament
  • Hit Fantasy League Tracker to follow points through the season
